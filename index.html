How WrenchLink works

Two portals. One platform. Built for the way the auto industry actually operates.

Technician
1

Build Your Vault

Enter your certifications (ASE, I-CAR, OEM), specialties, work history, and salary expectations. Set your availability status — actively looking, passively open, or not available.

2

Join a City Pool

Select your metro area to be discovered by local employers. You control your visibility. Employers in your pool can view your profile and reach out proactively.

3

One-Click Apply

Browse matched jobs and apply instantly with your saved vault. No re-entering information, no cover letters — your work history does the talking.

4

Get Hired, Get Paid

Communicate directly with shops via secure in-platform messaging. Compare offers, negotiate salary, and accept — all in WrenchLink.

Employer
1

Create a Shop Profile

Showcase your shop — brand, specialties, certifications, culture, and compensation packages. Build trust with technicians before they apply.

2

Post Open Positions

Create detailed job listings with required certs, pay range, shift type, and required experience level. Reach technicians in your City Pool automatically.

3

Search & Filter Candidates

Search the full technician database by skill, cert, experience, location, and availability status. Save favorites and build shortlists.

4

Reach Out Directly

Message candidates directly through WrenchLink's encrypted messaging system. Track applications, schedule interviews, and extend offers in one place.

Platform Features

Everything the trade needs

Purpose-built features for an industry that runs on skill, speed, and trust.

One-Click Apply

Technicians apply to any job instantly using their saved vault. No forms, no PDFs — just tap and apply.

Verified Credentials

ASE, I-CAR, OEM, and trade certifications are verified and displayed prominently on every profile.

City Pool Network

Geo-targeted talent pools connect local shops with local technicians — no relocation guesswork.

Direct Messaging

Employers and technicians communicate directly through encrypted in-platform messaging. No third-party required.

Rating System

Smart Candidate Search

Filter candidates by cert, specialty, years of experience, salary range, location, and live availability status.

Job Post Management

Employers manage all open positions, track applications, and monitor posting performance from one dashboard.

Availability Signals

Technicians set Active, Passive, or Not Looking status in real time — no more cold-messaging unavailable candidates.

Salary Transparency

Technicians set salary targets. Employers post real pay ranges. No surprises at the offer stage.
