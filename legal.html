Privacy Policy

Effective June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WrenchLink collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the Platform.

Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email, password, account type, and city pool.
  • Profile information — for Technicians: certifications, work history, education, references, salary expectations, availability, and uploaded documents. For Employers: business details, job postings, and payment information.
  • Usage data — pages visited, searches, applications, and messages, used to operate and improve the Platform.
  • Payment data — processed by our third-party payment processor; we store only limited details such as card brand and last four digits.

How we use information

  • To provide the matching, messaging, and subscription services.
  • To surface Technician profiles to Employers in the relevant City Pool, according to your visibility settings.
  • To process payments and send service, billing, and security communications.
  • To detect fraud, enforce our Terms, and comply with law.

How we share information

We share profile information with Employers as part of the core service, and with service providers (hosting, payments, analytics) under contract. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Your choices

You can edit or delete your profile, set your availability to "Not Looking" to limit visibility, request a copy of your data, or request deletion of your account by contacting privacy@wrenchlink.io. Depending on your location, you may have rights under the CCPA, GDPR, or other laws.

Data retention & security

We retain information while your account is active and as needed to comply with legal obligations. We use administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data, though no system is perfectly secure.

Cookie Policy

Effective June 19, 2026

WrenchLink uses cookies and similar technologies (including browser local storage) to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how the Platform is used.

Types we use

  • Strictly necessary — authentication and session state required for the Platform to function.
  • Functional — remembering settings such as your selected City Pool and availability status.
  • Analytics — aggregated, privacy-respecting usage measurement to improve the product.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent core features such as signing in from working. This demo build stores your session in your browser's local storage and does not set third-party advertising cookies.

Billing, Subscriptions & Refunds

Effective June 19, 2026

Subscriptions

WrenchLink offers recurring monthly subscriptions. Technician "Solo" accounts are $9.95/month. Employer plans are $99 (Starter), $249 (Pro), and custom (Enterprise) per month. By subscribing, you authorize WrenchLink to charge your payment method automatically each month until you cancel.

Billing cycle

Your subscription renews automatically on the same day each month (your billing anchor date). We generate an invoice for each cycle, which is available in your Billing & Invoices page. If a charge fails, we may retry and will mark the account "past due" until payment is received.

Cancellation

You may cancel at any time from your Billing page. Cancellation stops future charges; your access continues until the end of the current paid period. We do not charge cancellation fees.

Refunds

Monthly subscription fees are generally non-refundable, except where required by law. If you believe you were charged in error, contact billing@wrenchlink.io within 30 days and we will review in good faith.

Taxes & price changes

Prices exclude applicable taxes, which may be added at checkout. We may change subscription prices with at least 30 days' notice; changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
